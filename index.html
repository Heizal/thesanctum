About the Sanctum.

The Sanctum (an apartment hotel) is a private company that has been in existence since January 2009. We offer accommodation, conference, food and beverage and banqueting services.

  • We are an apartment hotel built to suit individuals, corporates and long term holiday makers who seek a quiet, comfortable and truly authentic African environment.t.
  • We currently have got 45 spacious apartments each with a kitchenette, living room, bedroom and a unique washroom and wireless internet.
  • The menu ranges from A’la Carte (order by the card), children's menu to (self-service–banqueting) buffet Menu. The Sanctum hotel also operates a shuttle for airport transfers at an extra charge..

Our Conference facility at Sanctum hotel is the right thing you need for your ball-room banquets, Corporate meetings and relatively, equally, big functions. We also have an Olympic size swimming pool and SPA. The hotel has also got the only snooker bar in Uganda. Our well equipped kitchen with an orientation of African, European and Continental Cuisines produces well filling and seditious healthy delicious dishes.
